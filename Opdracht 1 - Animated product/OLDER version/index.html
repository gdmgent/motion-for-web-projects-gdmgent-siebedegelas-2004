What are fidget spinners?

Fidget spinners are a type of stress reliever. They are small, usually ball-bearing devices that have a bearing in the center, which allows them to spin on an axis. They are often made of metal, but can also be made of plastic or wood. They are often used for entertainment purposes, but can also be used for therapeutic purposes.
